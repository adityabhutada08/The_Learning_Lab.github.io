Lessons To Be Covered!!

Learning the letter Sounds

The main 42 sounds of English are taught - one day every sound.

Learning the Letter Formation

As the letter Sound are Introduced , Students are taught to form each letter correctly.

Blending

Sound Learning is Okay , but students are taught to blend letter correctly.
e.g : d-o-g = dog

Identifying Sounds in Words

It is essential that children can hear the individual sound in words , especially for Writing. Initially Students were ask to hear it Properly now they are taught to identify Sound in words.
e.g: Is there ' S ' in .... "SUN" ... "MOUSE" ... "DOG" ?

Tricky Words

After first Month Students are able to identify 18 letter sound and their blending . Now they are ready to learn tricky words, this are those words which cannot always work by blending.
e.g. "was" has /O/ sound instead of /a/ sound.
